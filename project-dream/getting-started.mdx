## Installation

Follow these steps to set up Project Dream.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    Sign up at the Pulze website and enable Project Dream for your workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your workspace">
    Configure your project settings and preferences.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your first project">
    Use the Project Dream interface to generate your first creative project.
  </Step>
</Steps>
