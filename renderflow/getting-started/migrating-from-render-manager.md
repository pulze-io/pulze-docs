Render Manager is deprecated and will no longer receive updates or bug fixes. RenderFlow is its successor, built from the ground up with a modern architecture, more features, and support for additional applications and workflows.

## Before you upgrade

Test RenderFlow with your production scenes before committing to the switch. You can request a [30-day free trial](https://www.pulze.io) to evaluate it alongside your existing Render Manager setup.

<Warning>
Upgrading your subscription converts your Render Manager licenses to RenderFlow licenses. This cannot be reversed. Always test first.
</Warning>

## How to upgrade

1. Visit your [Pulze account page](https://www.pulze.io/account).
2. Click **Upgrade to RenderFlow** (or **Upgrade to Workflow Bundle** if you have a Creators Bundle).
3. Review the popup message for upgrade details and confirm.

The upgrade is free. Your subscription price remains unchanged until you cancel. Starter licenses are converted to RenderFlow licenses automatically.

If you had a **Creators Bundle** (Scene Manager + Render Manager), it becomes a **Workflow Bundle** (Scene Manager + RenderFlow) at the same price.

## After upgrading

Install RenderFlow on your server and render nodes following the [Quick Start](/renderflow/getting-started/quick-start) guide. RenderFlow is a new application and does not share configuration or job history with Render Manager. You will set up your server, connect your nodes, and start submitting jobs fresh.

  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I have a Creators Bundle. How do I upgrade?">
    The Creators Bundle becomes a Workflow Bundle (Scene Manager + RenderFlow). Click **Upgrade to Workflow Bundle** on your account page. The upgrade is free.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Will my starter licenses transfer?">
    Yes. All subscriptions that include a starter license will have it transferred after the upgrade.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How much does it cost?">
    The upgrade is free for active Render Manager subscribers. Your subscription price stays the same until you cancel.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need help?">
    If you need additional licenses or assistance with the migration, contact [support@pulze.io](mailto:support@pulze.io).
  </Accordion>
