## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Pulze?">
    Pulze is a suite of creative and developer tools including Scene Manager, RenderFlow, and Project Dream. Each product is designed to streamline a specific part of your creative workflow.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I get started?">
    Pick the product that fits your needs and follow its getting started guide. You can find links to each product on the [home page](/).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there an API?">
    Yes — RenderFlow provides a full REST API for automating render jobs. See the [REST API reference](/renderflow/developers/rest-api) and [developer docs](/renderflow/developers/authentication) for details.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How can I get support?">
    Reach out to us at [support@pulze.io](mailto:support@pulze.io) or join our [Discord community](https://discord.gg/BxtBs9aN4E).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What platforms are supported?">
    Each product has its own platform support. Check the getting started guide for the product you're interested in for specific requirements.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
