## Installation

Follow these steps to set up RenderFlow.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    Sign up at the Pulze website and enable RenderFlow for your workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install the plugin or CLI">
    Download the RenderFlow plugin for your DCC tool, or install the CLI.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit your first render">
    Configure your render settings and submit a job to RenderFlow.
  </Step>
</Steps>
