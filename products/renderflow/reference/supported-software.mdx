## 3ds Max

**Supported versions:** 2019, 2020, 2021, 2022, 2023, 2024, 2025, 2026

**Render engines:** Arnold, Corona, FStorm, Octane, Redshift, V-Ray

### Detected plugins

RenderFlow searches for and tracks these plugins for requirement matching:

Anima, BerconMaps, CityTraffic, CloneModifier, ColorCorrect, ColorEdge, ComplexFresnel, FloorGenerator, ForestPack Pro, Glue, GrowFX, Laubwerk Player, MadCar, MultiScatter, MultiTexture, Ornatrix, Phoenix FD, Psd Manager, RailClone Pro, ReDeform, RichDirt 2, SigerNoise, SigerScratches, SiNi Software, SmartRefs, SplineOffset, Substance, ThinFilm, tyFlow, VRayPattern

## Blender

**Supported versions:** 3.0, 3.1, 3.2, 3.3 (LTS), 3.4, 3.5, 3.6 (LTS), 4.0, 4.1, 4.2 (LTS), 4.3, 4.4, 4.5 (LTS), 4.6, 5.0

**Render engines:** Cycles, EEVEE, V-Ray

## Cinema 4D

**Supported versions:** 2023, 2024, 2024.5, 2025, 2026

**Render engines:** Arnold, Corona, Octane, Physical, Redshift, Standard, V-Ray

## Fusion

**Supported versions:** 17, 18, 19, 20, 21

Also supports Fusion Render Node for distributed rendering.

## V-Ray Standalone

RenderFlow detects any installed version of V-Ray Standalone and can render `.vrscene` files directly.

## Other detected software

RenderFlow detects the following software for analytics and comparison purposes. You cannot create jobs for these applications, but they appear in Software Analytics for tracking installations across your farm.

- After Effects
- Arnold
- Houdini
- KeyShot
- Maya
- Nuke
- Photoshop
- Revit
- Rhino
- SketchUp
- Topaz Video AI
- Unreal Engine
- Vantage
