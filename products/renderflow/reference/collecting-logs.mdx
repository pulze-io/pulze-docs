When you encounter an issue you can't resolve, our support team is here to help. This guide shows you how to collect the information we need.

## Quick method: Error report collector

The fastest way to gather logs from multiple computers at once.

1. Go to **Menu → Support → Error report collector**
2. Select the nodes experiencing issues
3. Optionally select related jobs (helps narrow down the problem)
4. Click **Collect**
5. Send the generated archive to support

## Manual collection

If the collector isn't available, find logs in these locations:

| Log Type | Location |
|----------|----------|
| Service log | `C:\ProgramData\RenderFlow\logs\service-YYYY-MM-DD.log` |
| Database log | `C:\ProgramData\RenderFlow\logs\database-YYYY-MM-DD.log` |
| Job logs | `C:\ProgramData\RenderFlow\logs\<jobid>.log` |
| 3ds Max plugin log | `%TEMP%\RenderFlowMax.YYYYMMDD.log` |
| 3ds Max network log | `%LOCALAPPDATA%\Autodesk\3dsMax\<version>\ENU\Network\Max.log` |

## Viewing logs in RenderFlow

### Service logs

View your computer's logs directly in the app:

**Menu → Support → Open logs**

<Frame caption="Service log viewer">
  <img src="https://placehold.co/900x400?text=Service+Log+Viewer" alt="Service log viewer" />
</Frame>

### Job logs

When a task fails, check the job logs:

1. Open the job details
2. Find the **Nodes** section
3. Click **Open logs**
4. Filter, select, and copy or save logs as needed
